Tacoma Topper Roof Rack

Constraints & Goals

  • Durable, load-bearing, low profile with a professional look
  • 4× cross members, each mounted to rails on the topper
  • Custom bent 5052 aluminum brackets
  • Extruded aluminum cross members for easy attachment mounting

Post-Mortem

  • Durability achieved; $430 spent vs. comparable retail product at >$800
  • Rail curvature on topper means cross members are not perfectly flat — spacers can be added later if needed. 8× robust attachment points preferred over 4× flat points.

Truck Bed Box

Constraints & Goals

  • Protect and house permanent gear in the truck bed
  • Made from ½″ and ¾″ plywood with a lightweight design
  • Internal structures to hold gear snug off-road
  • Platform near tailgate for water cube

Post-Mortem

  • Holds gear very well; truck bed liner kept the box inconspicuous
  • Tinted topper windows make box difficult to see from outside
  • Accessing gear via side window and trapdoor proved mildly inconvenient

Tacoma Truck Bed Drawers

Constraints & Goals

Modular Guitar Pedalboard

Constraints & Goals

  • Small enough to 3D print; pedals connect to each other modularly
  • Infinitely expandable; room for wiring underneath

Post-Mortem

  • Held pedals nicely; sold 20 units online
  • Velcro would have looked cleaner than tape for securing pedals
  • Non-standard pedal sizes required re-measuring for new prints
  • Bottom rubber feet wore out faster than expected
