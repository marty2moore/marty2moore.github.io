Bertec Portable Pitching Mound

Bertec Portable Pitching Mound Website

Led mechanical design and development for Bertec's Portable Pitching Mound, a biomechanics system for baseball performance analysis. Designed for portability, precision, and durability in both lab and field environments. Responsibilities included full design of system - allowing for existing force plates to be used within the system, complete manufacturing documentation, and supporting product and marketing team for full completion of project.

Bertec Walkway

Bertec Walkway Brochure

Led mechanical design for the Bertec Fixed Walkway, a biomechanics system primarily targeted for researchers to use for gait analysis. The main goal with this design was modularity; one set of mounting plates will work with all desired walkway setups. If the force plate setup needs to be changed, force plates and filler plates can be swapped around for optimal data collection. Plates can be used interchangably in a way that the user does not know where the force or filler plates are located.
