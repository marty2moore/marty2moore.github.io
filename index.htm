About Me

Hello, I’m Martin Moore. I’m an engineer with experience in mechanical design, product development, prototyping, testing, and production support. My work has included designing clinical and sports performance systems at Bertec, supporting manufacturing and quality processes at GE Aviation, and contributing to research on magnetic gearing at Ohio State. I’ve used tools like SolidWorks, Python, and MATLAB to improve designs, analyze performance, and support production. I studied mechanical engineering at The Ohio State University. Outside of work, I enjoy climbing, biking, and hands-on projects.

Professional Portfolio

Bertec Portable Pitching Mound

Led mechanical design and development for Bertec's Portable Pitching Mound, a biomechanics system for baseball performance analysis. Designed for portability, precision, and durability in both lab and field environments. Responsibilities included full design of system - allowing for existing force plates to be used within the system, complete manufacturing documentation, and supporting product and marketing team for full completion of project.
