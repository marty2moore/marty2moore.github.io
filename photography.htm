Yashica FX-3 2000

Camera Facts

  • 35mm manual-focus film SLR camera
  • Introduced in the late 1980s
  • Fully mechanical shutter – operates without batteries
  • Built-in light meter
  • Lightweight and compact body (for the time)
  • Manual film advance and rewind

Kodak Brownie Hawkeye

Camera Facts

  • Introduced in 1949 – My model is from 1954
  • Made by Eastman Kodak Company
  • Box-style film camera
  • Uses 620 roll film (Medium format – need to 3D print film rolls to fit properly in camera)
  • Fixed-focus single-element meniscus lens
  • Rotary shutter with a speed around 1/30 second
  • Waist-level viewfinder for framing shots – see above image to see what it looks like when taking a picture
  • Very popular among amateur photographers
  • Still collected and modified by film enthusiasts today
