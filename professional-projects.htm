Bertec Portable Pitching Mound

Bertec Portable Pitching Mound Website →

Led mechanical design and development for Bertec's Portable Pitching Mound, a biomechanics system for baseball performance analysis. Designed for portability, precision, and durability in both lab and field environments. Responsibilities included full system design, complete manufacturing documentation, and supporting the product and marketing team through launch.

Bertec Walkway

Bertec Walkway Brochure (PDF) →

Led mechanical design for the Bertec Fixed Walkway, a biomechanics system primarily for gait analysis research. The central design goal was modularity — one set of mounting plates accommodates all desired walkway configurations. Force plates and filler plates can be swapped interchangeably so researchers cannot distinguish plate locations, ensuring unbiased data collection.
